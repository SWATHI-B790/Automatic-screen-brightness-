Welcome to the Automatic Screen Brightness App

The Automatic Screen Brightness app adjusts the brightness of your device screen based on your environment's lighting conditions, providing optimal viewing comfort while reducing eye strain.

With this app, your device will intelligently adapt its screen brightness to match ambient lighting, ensuring you have a comfortable viewing experience, whether in a dark room or under bright sunlight.

Protect Your Eyes: Constant exposure to bright screens in low-light conditions can cause eye strain and discomfort. This app helps reduce this by lowering the screen brightness in dark environments, preventing eye fatigue and improving comfort.

About Automatic Screen Brightness

Automatic Screen Brightness is a technology that adjusts the screen brightness of your device based on the surrounding light. This reduces power consumption and provides a better viewing experience by ensuring the screen is neither too bright nor too dim for the lighting conditions.

The system uses sensors, usually located near the camera or on the front of the device, to measure the ambient light levels. It then adjusts the screen brightness in real-time, optimizing visibility while also saving battery life, especially when you're in low-light conditions.
